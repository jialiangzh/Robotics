Welcome to Our Robotic Pet Project!

Introduction

Our project aims to bring convenience and entertainment to our everyday life. An ideal robotic pet can follow the master like a servant and can perform real time communication with the master. The robotic pet should also resemble a real pet and bring enjoyment to master's free time. In our project, we specifically investigate on implementing two major functionalities for our robot pet: hand gesture tracking and self entertaining with a ball. We want it to be live!

Implementation

Hardware

Raspberry Pi with camera, rPi extension board, Tetrix Robot frames and wheels, Tetrix DC motor and 12V Battery

Software
